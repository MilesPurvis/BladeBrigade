FAQs About Drones

What are drones?

Drones, also known as unmanned aerial vehicles (UAVs), are aircraft without a human pilot aboard. They can be controlled remotely by a human operator or autonomously by onboard computers.

What are the uses of drones?

Drones have various applications including aerial photography, videography, surveillance, agriculture, search and rescue operations, package delivery, and recreational activities.

Are there any regulations for flying drones?

Yes, there are regulations and guidelines set by aviation authorities in different countries to ensure safe and responsible drone operation. These regulations typically cover aspects such as drone registration, flight restrictions, altitude limits, and privacy concerns.
